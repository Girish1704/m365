### 1. PURPOSE

This policy establishes guidelines for business travel and expense reimbursement at Contoso Corporation. It ensures that employees can conduct necessary business travel while maintaining fiscal responsibility and compliance with company standards.

---

### 2. SCOPE

This policy applies to:
- All full-time and part-time employees
- Contractors and consultants (where specified)
- Company-sponsored travel for candidates and guests
- All domestic and international business travel

---

### 3. GENERAL PRINCIPLES

- Travel should be approved before booking
- Choose cost-effective options that meet business needs
- Submit expense reports within 30 days of travel
- Maintain receipts for all expenses over $25
- Personal expenses must not be included
- Comply with all tax and legal requirements

---

### 4. TRAVEL APPROVAL

#### 4.1 Approval Requirements

| Travel Type | Approval Required |
|-------------|------------------|
| Local (within 50 miles) | No approval needed |
| Domestic (overnight) | Manager approval |
| International | VP-level approval |
| Team/Conference travel | Department Head approval |

#### 4.2 Advance Booking

- Book travel at least **14 days** in advance when possible
- Early booking typically results in 20-40% savings
- Last-minute bookings require manager justification

---

### 5. AIR TRAVEL

#### 5.1 Booking Guidelines

| Flight Duration | Class Permitted |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Under 6 hours | Economy |
| 6+ hours | Premium Economy |
| 8+ hours (international) | Business Class (VP+ only) |

#### 5.2 Booking Procedures

- Use the approved corporate travel portal (travel.contoso.com)
- Compare prices across airlines for best value
- Choose refundable fares when schedule is uncertain
- Seat selection and one checked bag are reimbursable

#### 5.3 Frequent Flyer Programs

- Employees may retain personal frequent flyer miles
- Do not book more expensive flights to earn miles
- Corporate travel accounts take priority for savings

---

### 6. GROUND TRANSPORTATION

#### 6.1 Rental Vehicles

| Category | When Permitted |
|----------|---------------|
| Economy/Compact | Standard rentals |
| Intermediate | 3+ passengers or heavy luggage |
| Full-size/SUV | 4+ passengers or special circumstances |

**Requirements:**
- Decline rental company insurance (covered by Contoso)
- Refuel before returning to avoid surcharges
- Report accidents immediately

#### 6.2 Personal Vehicle Use

| Mileage Rate | Amount |
|--------------|--------|
| Standard IRS Rate | $0.67 per mile (2025) |
| Maximum without approval | 200 miles round trip |

**Reimbursable:**
- Mileage from office to destination
- Parking at business location
- Tolls on direct route

**Not Reimbursable:**
- Commute from home to regular office
- Traffic violations or parking tickets
- Personal errands during business trips

#### 6.3 Taxi, Rideshare, and Public Transit

| Transportation | Guideline |
|----------------|-----------|
| Airport transfers | Taxi/rideshare reimbursed |
| Local travel | Use judgment for cost-effectiveness |
| Public transit | Encouraged when practical |
| Limousine service | Not permitted without approval |

---

### 7. LODGING

#### 7.1 Hotel Guidelines

| Location | Per Night Maximum |
|----------|------------------|
| Tier 1 Cities (NYC, SF, Boston, DC) | $300 |
| Tier 2 Cities | $225 |
| Other Locations | $175 |
| Extended Stay (5+ nights) | 15% discount expected |

#### 7.2 Preferred Hotels

Book through the corporate portal to access:
- Negotiated corporate rates
- Contoso rewards program
- Streamlined billing

#### 7.3 Room Standards

- Standard room (single occupancy)
- Upgrades at employee expense unless offered free
- Cancel reservations 24+ hours in advance to avoid fees

---

#### 8.1 Daily Meal Allowances

| Location Type | Daily Maximum |
|---------------|---------------|
| Tier 1 Cities | $100 |
| Tier 2 Cities | $80 |
| Other Locations | $65 |
| International | Actual & reasonable |

#### 8.2 Individual Meals (Approximate Guidelines)

| Meal | Guideline |
|------|-----------|
| Breakfast | $20 |
| Lunch | $25 |
| Dinner | $40-55 |

#### 8.3 Business Entertainment

| Entertainment Type | Approval | Maximum |
|--------------------|----------|---------|
| Client meals (1-2 guests) | Manager | $75/person |
| Client meals (3+ guests) | Director | $75/person |

- List all attendees and company affiliations
- No alcohol during working hours
- Reasonable and appropriate choices

|---------|--------|
| Baggage fees | First checked bag |
| Wi-Fi (flights/hotels) | When needed for work |
| Business phone calls | Actual cost |
| Currency exchange | Reasonable fees |
| Passport/visa fees | For required business travel |
| Vaccinations | For required international travel |
| Laundry | Trips over 5 days |
| Tips | 15-20% customary |

---

### 10. NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES

The following expenses will **not** be reimbursed:

- Personal entertainment (movies, books, games)
- Mini-bar charges
- In-room movies
- Personal phone calls (except brief family check-ins)
- Gym or spa services
- Traffic violations or parking tickets
- Travel insurance (covered by Contoso)
- Expenses for spouses/companions
- Upgrades beyond policy (airline, hotel, car)
- Childcare or pet care
- Lost or stolen personal items
- Clothing or personal items
- Alcoholic beverages (except client entertainment)

---

### 11. INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL

#### 11.1 Planning Requirements

- Submit request 30 days in advance
- Obtain necessary visas and travel documents
- Review travel advisories (travel.state.gov)
- Complete international travel training
- Register with Contoso Global Security

#### 11.2 Additional Allowances

| Item | Policy |
|------|--------|
| International phone plan | Covered or reimbursed |
| Currency exchange | Reasonable fees covered |
| ATM fees | Covered abroad |
| Travel insurance | Provided by Contoso |
| Emergency medical | Covered by corporate policy |

#### 11.3 Per Diem Rates

International per diem follows U.S. Department of State rates for the destination city. Check travel.contoso.com for current rates.

---

### 12. EXPENSE REPORTING

#### 12.1 Submission Process

1. Use Contoso Expense Portal (expense.contoso.com)
2. Submit within **30 days** of expense or trip completion
3. Attach itemized receipts for expenses over $25
4. Include business justification for each expense
5. Manager approval required before processing

#### 12.2 Receipt Requirements

| Expense Amount | Receipt Required |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Under $25 | Optional (log required) |
| $25 - $75 | Standard receipt |
| Over $75 | Itemized receipt |

**Acceptable Receipts:**
- Original receipts (scanned)
- Credit card statements with itemization
- Electronic receipts

#### 12.3 Processing Time

| Submission | Reimbursement Timeline |
|------------|----------------------|
| Complete with receipts | 7-10 business days |
| Missing information | Delayed until resolved |
| Over 60 days old | Requires VP approval |
| Over 90 days old | May be denied |

---

### 13. CORPORATE CREDIT CARD

#### 13.1 Eligibility

Corporate cards are issued to employees who:
- Travel regularly (4+ trips per year)
- Have manager approval
- Complete cardholder agreement

#### 13.2 Card Usage

**Permitted:**
- Business travel expenses
- Client entertainment
- Approved business purchases

**Not Permitted:**
- Personal purchases
- Cash advances (without approval)
- Purchases outside policy limits

#### 13.3 Cardholder Responsibilities

- Maintain physical card security
- Report lost/stolen cards immediately
- Reconcile statements monthly
- Submit expenses within 30 days

---

### 14. TRAVEL SAFETY

#### 14.1 Before You Travel

- Share itinerary with manager and emergency contact
- Register international trips with Global Security
- Review destination safety information
- Download Contoso Traveler app

#### 14.2 During Travel

- Keep copies of important documents
- Use hotel safes for valuables
- Be aware of surroundings
- Follow local laws and customs
