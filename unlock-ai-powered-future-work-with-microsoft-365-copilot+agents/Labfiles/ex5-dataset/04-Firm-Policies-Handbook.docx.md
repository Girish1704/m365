## SECTION 2: OFFICE HOURS & ATTENDANCE

### Standard Business Hours
- Monday - Friday: 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM
- Attorneys: Flexible based on client needs
- Support Staff: Fixed schedule unless approved

### Time Tracking
- All billable time must be recorded daily
- Use the firm's TimeTracker Pro software
- Minimum billing increment: 6 minutes (0.1 hour)
- Submit timesheets by 5 PM each Friday

### Remote Work Policy
- Attorneys: Up to 2 days per week (with partner approval)
- Paralegals: Up to 1 day per week
- Support Staff: Case-by-case basis
- Must be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM)

- Bi-weekly (every other Friday)

## SECTION 5: PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT

### Confidentiality
- All client matters are strictly confidential
- Never discuss cases in public areas
- Lock screens when away from desk
- Secure all physical documents

### Conflicts of Interest
- Run conflict checks before accepting any matter
- Report potential conflicts immediately to Managing Partner
- Maintain complete conflict database entries

### Client Communication
- Respond to client inquiries within 24 hours
- Document all substantive communications
- Provide monthly status updates on active matters
- Obtain approval for any communication with opposing counsel
