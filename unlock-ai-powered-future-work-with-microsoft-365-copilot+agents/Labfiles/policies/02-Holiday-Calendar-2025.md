### 1. PURPOSE

This document outlines the official company holidays observed by Contoso Corporation for the calendar year 2025. All offices and facilities will be closed on these dates unless otherwise specified.

---

### 2. SCOPE

This holiday calendar applies to all Contoso Corporation employees in the United States. International offices should refer to their regional holiday calendars.

---

### 5. FLOATING HOLIDAYS

All employees are entitled to **2 floating holidays** per calendar year, which may be used for:

- Religious observances not covered by official holidays
- Cultural celebrations
- Personal significance days
- Any other purpose at employee discretion

**Guidelines:**
- Floating holidays must be requested at least 5 business days in advance
- Floating holidays do not carry over to the next calendar year
- Unused floating holidays are not paid out upon termination

---

### 6. HOLIDAY PAY

#### 6.1 Eligible Employees

All regular full-time employees receive holiday pay at their regular rate for official company holidays.

#### 6.2 Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees receive prorated holiday pay based on their average weekly hours.

#### 6.3 Working on Holidays

Employees required to work on official holidays will receive:
- Regular pay for hours worked, PLUS
- Compensatory time off (1.5x hours worked) to be taken within 60 days

### 8. RELIGIOUS ACCOMMODATIONS

Contoso Corporation respects the religious practices of all employees. Employees requiring time off for religious observances not covered by official holidays may:

1. Use floating holidays
2. Use personal leave
3. Request schedule adjustments with manager approval
4. Request unpaid time off if other options are exhausted

Please contact Human Resources to discuss accommodation requests.
