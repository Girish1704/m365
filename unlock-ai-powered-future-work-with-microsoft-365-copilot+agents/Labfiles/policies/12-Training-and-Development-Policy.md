# CONTOSO CORPORATION

## TRAINING AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT POLICY

**Document Number:** HR-POL-012  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Reviewed:** November 25, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** Learning & Development Department  
**Approved By:** Sarah Johnson, Vice President of Talent Management

---

### 1. PURPOSE

Contoso Corporation is committed to the continuous growth and development of our employees. This policy establishes guidelines for training programs, professional development opportunities, and educational assistance to help employees enhance their skills and advance their careers.

---

### 2. SCOPE

This policy applies to:
- All full-time employees (immediately eligible)
- Part-time employees (eligible after 6 months)
- Certain benefits require tenure or role-specific criteria

---

### 3. LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT PHILOSOPHY

Contoso believes that:
- Continuous learning drives innovation and success
- Employees own their development with company support
- Skills growth benefits both individuals and the organization
- Diverse learning methods accommodate different needs
- Development is a shared investment

---

### 4. TRAINING PROGRAMS

#### 4.1 Mandatory Training

All employees must complete required training:

| Training | Frequency | Due Date |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Code of Conduct | Annual | January 31 |
| Security Awareness | Quarterly | End of each quarter |
| Anti-Harassment | Biennial | Within 30 days of hire + every 2 years |
| Data Privacy | Annual | March 31 |
| Safety Training | Annual | Upon hire + annually |

Completion is tracked in Contoso Learning Portal.

#### 4.2 Role-Based Training

| Role Category | Training Focus |
|--------------|----------------|
| Individual Contributors | Technical skills, productivity, collaboration |
| Managers | Leadership, coaching, performance management |
| Senior Leaders | Executive development, strategy, change management |
| Customer-Facing | Product knowledge, customer service, communication |
| Technical Roles | Certifications, emerging technologies, best practices |

#### 4.3 Learning Resources

| Resource | Description | Access |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Contoso Learning Portal | Internal courses and curriculum | learn.contoso.com |
| LinkedIn Learning | 16,000+ online courses | Company subscription |
| Technical Platforms | Pluralsight, Coursera, etc. | By approval |
| Instructor-Led Training | Classroom and virtual sessions | Learning Portal |
| Mentoring Program | 1:1 guidance from senior employees | HR facilitated |

---

### 5. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

#### 5.1 Individual Development Plans (IDPs)

All employees should have an IDP that includes:
- Career goals (short and long-term)
- Skills to develop
- Learning activities planned
- Timeline and milestones
- Manager support needed

IDPs are reviewed during quarterly check-ins.

#### 5.2 Career Conversations

| Conversation | Frequency | Participants |
|--------------|-----------|--------------|
| Development Check-ins | Quarterly | Employee + Manager |
| Career Planning | Biannual | Employee + Manager + HR |
| Talent Reviews | Annual | Leadership team |

#### 5.3 Career Paths

Contoso supports multiple career paths:
- **Technical Track:** Individual contributor growth
- **Management Track:** People leadership
- **Expert Track:** Subject matter expertise
- **Cross-Functional:** Lateral moves to build breadth

---

### 6. TUITION REIMBURSEMENT

#### 6.1 Eligibility

- Full-time employees with 1+ year of service
- Good performance standing
- Manager and HR approval
- Course relates to current role or career path

#### 6.2 Reimbursement Amounts

| Education Level | Annual Maximum |
|-----------------|----------------|
| Undergraduate degree | $5,250 |
| Graduate degree | $10,000 |
| Professional certificates | $3,000 |
| Individual courses | $2,000 |

#### 6.3 Approved Expenses

| Covered | Not Covered |
|---------|-------------|
| Tuition and fees | Room and board |
| Required textbooks | Travel costs |
| Lab fees | Personal expenses |
| Online course fees | Application fees |

#### 6.4 Grade Requirements

| Grade | Reimbursement |
|-------|---------------|
| A or B (or equivalent) | 100% |
| C | 75% |
| Below C or Incomplete | 0% |
| Pass/Fail (Pass) | 100% |

#### 6.5 Repayment Agreement

Employees receiving tuition reimbursement agree to:
- Remain employed for 12 months after course completion
- Repay pro-rated amount if leaving voluntarily
- Exception: Involuntary termination without cause

### 7. PROFESSIONAL CERTIFICATIONS

#### 7.1 Certification Support

| Support Type | Details |
|--------------|---------|
| Exam fees | Covered for approved certifications |
| Study materials | Up to $500 per certification |
| Study time | Up to 8 hours PTO for preparation |
| Retake fees | One retake covered |

#### 7.2 Approved Certifications

Certifications must be:
- Relevant to current or target role
- From recognized certification bodies
- Approved by manager before pursuit

**Examples of supported certifications:**
- Project Management (PMP, CAPM)
- Agile/Scrum (CSM, PSM)
- Cloud (Azure, AWS, GCP)
- Security (CISSP, Security+)
- HR (SHRM, PHR)
- Finance (CPA, CFA)
- Microsoft (365, Power Platform, Azure)

#### 7.3 Certification Bonuses

| Certification Level | One-Time Bonus |
|--------------------|----------------|
| Associate/Entry | $250 |
| Professional | $500 |
| Expert/Advanced | $1,000 |

### 9. MENTORING PROGRAM

#### 9.1 Program Structure

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Duration | 6-12 months |
| Meeting frequency | At least monthly |
| Matching | Based on goals and experience |
| Training | Provided for mentors and mentees |

#### 9.2 Mentor Responsibilities

- Share knowledge and experience
- Provide guidance and feedback
- Support mentee's development goals
- Maintain confidentiality
- Commit to regular meetings

#### 9.3 Mentee Responsibilities

- Drive the relationship
- Come prepared to meetings
- Be open to feedback
- Follow through on commitments
- Respect mentor's time

---

### 10. JOB SHADOWING AND ROTATIONS

#### 10.1 Job Shadowing

- Short-term observation (1-5 days)
- Learn about different roles and departments
- Manager approval required
- Available to all employees

#### 10.2 Developmental Rotations

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Duration | 3-6 months |
| Eligibility | 18+ months tenure, good standing |
| Process | Application and manager approval |
| Compensation | No change during rotation |

---

### 11. LEARNING TIME

#### 11.1 Dedicated Learning Time

Employees are encouraged to dedicate time to learning:

| Role Level | Recommended Learning Time |
|------------|--------------------------|
| Individual Contributor | 2-4 hours per month |
| Manager | 4-6 hours per month |
| Senior Leader | 6-8 hours per month |

#### 11.2 Learning During Work Hours

- Mandatory training: During work hours
- Job-relevant learning: During work hours with manager approval
- Personal development: Personal time or flex arrangements

---

### 12. MANAGER RESPONSIBILITIES

Managers play a critical role in employee development:

- Have regular career conversations
- Support IDP creation and execution
- Approve and facilitate learning opportunities
- Provide stretch assignments
- Give developmental feedback
- Model continuous learning

---

### 13. REQUESTING DEVELOPMENT SUPPORT

#### 13.1 Process

1. Discuss development goals with manager
2. Identify appropriate learning activity
3. Submit request through Learning Portal
4. Obtain manager approval
5. Complete activity
6. Submit reimbursement (if applicable)
7. Share learnings with team

#### 13.2 Approval Criteria

Requests are evaluated based on:
- Relevance to role or career path
- Business impact
- Cost and time investment
- Department budget availability
- Past learning completion record

---

### 14. MEASUREMENT AND TRACKING

#### 14.1 Learning Metrics

Contoso tracks:
- Training completion rates
- Hours of learning per employee
- Certification achievements
- Program satisfaction scores
- Business impact of training

#### 14.2 Recognition

Outstanding learners are recognized through:
- Learning Achievement Awards (quarterly)
- Certification announcements
- Development success stories
- Performance review acknowledgment
