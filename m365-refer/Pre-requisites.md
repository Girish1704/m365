# Pre-requisites of Mission to Transform Contoso Corp with Microsoft365 Copilot and Agents

#### Estimated Duration : 10 minutes

## Overview

This prerequisite lab focuses on using SharePoint to streamline the processes and enhance team collaboration. Participants will learn to create a SharePoint site named Contoso Corp, leveraging the team site template to centralize resources and upload all the related documents needed for the exercises. These tasks serve as essential foundational steps, demonstrating how SharePoint can effectively support collaborative workflows while showcasing the utility of AI tools in managing marketing operations.

## Lab Objectives

In this lab, you will complete the following tasks:

+ Task 1: Create the Contoso Corp Site in SharePoint

### Task 1: Create the Contoso Corp Site in SharePoint.

1. On your lab VM, open **Microsoft edge** browser.

   ![image](../media/pre1.png)

1. Navigate to [M365.cloud.microsoft](https://m365.cloud.microsoft/) in egde browser.

1. On the "Welcome to the Microsoft 365 Copilot App" page, select **Sign in** to proceed.

   ![image](../media/pre2.png)

1. On the **Sign in to Microsoft Azure** tab, you will see the login screen. Enter the following email or username, and click on **Next**.

   - **Email/Username:** <inject key="AzureAdUserEmail"></inject>
 
      ![image](../media/pre3.png)

1. Now enter the following password and click on **Sign in**.
 
   - **Password:** <inject key="AzureAdUserPassword"></inject>
 
      ![image](../media/pre4.png)

1. On the **M365 Copilot** portal, click on **App (1)** at the bottom, then click on **SharePoint (2)** to proceed.

   ![image](../media/pre5.png)

1. On the SharePoint homepage, click the **+ Create Site** button.

    ![image](../media/pre-6.png)

1. From the available options, choose **Team site** for the HR portal.

   ![image](../media/pre6.png)

1. Select the **Standard team** site template for a pre-designed layout.

   ![image](../media/pre7.png)

1. On the "Preview and use 'Standard team' template" page, click on **Use template** to proceed.
 
   ![image](../media/pre8.png)

1. Enter the name **Contoso Corp<inject key="DeploymentID" enableCopy="false"/> (1)**

1. Add a description like, **Central hub for Contoso**

1. Click on **Next**.

   ![image](../media/pre9.png)

1. On the **Set language and other options** page, change the privacy setting to **Public - anyone in the organizationcan access the site**, then click on **Create site** to complete the process and once created click on **Finish**.

   ![image](../media/pre10.png)

    >**Note**: If you see any prompt, simply click on **Close** to dismiss it.

1. Select the **Documents** blade from the left panel.

   ![image](../media/pre11.png)

1. Select the **Upload** tab from the top and click on **file** to upload.

   ![image](../media/pre12.png)

1. Navigate to the location **C:\LabFiles\Documents** and select all the files to upload and click on **open**.
