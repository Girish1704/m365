### 1. PURPOSE

This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the benefits available to Contoso Corporation employees. Our benefits package is designed to support your health, financial security, and overall well-being.

---

### 2. ELIGIBILITY

| Employment Status | Benefits Eligibility |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Full-time (30+ hours/week) | Full benefits package |
| Part-time (20-29 hours/week) | Medical, dental, 401(k) only |
| Part-time (<20 hours/week) | 401(k) only |
| Temporary/Contract | As specified in contract |

Benefits become effective on the **first day of the month** following your start date.

---

### 3. HEALTH INSURANCE

#### 3.1 Medical Coverage

Contoso offers three medical plan options through our provider, BlueCross BlueShield:

| Plan Type | Employee Cost (Monthly) | Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Max |
|-----------|------------------------|------------|-------------------|
| **Basic PPO** | $125 | $2,000 | $6,000 |
| **Standard PPO** | $225 | $1,000 | $4,000 |
| **Premium PPO** | $350 | $500 | $2,500 |

**Coverage Includes:**
- Preventive care (100% covered)
- Doctor visits and specialist consultations
- Hospital and emergency services
- Prescription drug coverage
- Mental health and substance abuse services
- Maternity and newborn care

#### 3.2 Dental Coverage

| Coverage Type | Annual Maximum | Employee Cost (Monthly) |
|---------------|----------------|------------------------|
| **Basic Dental** | $1,500 | $25 |
| **Premium Dental** | $3,000 | $45 |

**Coverage Includes:**
- Preventive care: 100% covered
- Basic services (fillings, extractions): 80% covered
- Major services (crowns, bridges): 50% covered
- Orthodontia (Premium only): 50% up to $2,000 lifetime

#### 3.3 Vision Coverage

| Coverage | Employee Cost (Monthly) |
|----------|------------------------|
| **Vision Plan** | $15 |

**Coverage Includes:**
- Annual eye exam: $10 copay
- Frames allowance: $150 every 24 months
- Contact lens allowance: $150 annually
- Lens options (progressive, transitions): discounted rates

### 4. RETIREMENT BENEFITS

#### 4.1 401(k) Plan

Contoso Corporation offers a comprehensive 401(k) retirement savings plan.

**Key Features:**

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Eligibility | Immediately upon hire |
| Employee Contribution Limit | Up to IRS maximum ($23,000 for 2025) |
| Catch-up Contributions (50+) | Additional $7,500 |
| Company Match | 100% of first 4% of salary |
| Vesting Schedule | Immediate for company match |

**Investment Options:**
- Target-date funds
- Index funds (S&P 500, Total Market, International)
- Bond funds
- Company stock (limited to 10% of portfolio)
- Self-directed brokerage account

#### 4.2 Pension Plan

Employees with 5+ years of service are eligible for the Contoso Pension Plan:
- Benefit: 1.5% of final average salary per year of service
- Normal retirement age: 65
- Early retirement available at 55 with reduced benefits

### 6. WELLNESS PROGRAMS

#### 6.1 Wellness Incentive Program

Earn up to **$500 annually** in wellness rewards by completing:
- Annual health assessment: $100
- Biometric screening: $100
- Fitness challenge participation: $100
- Preventive care visits: $100
- Wellness education courses: $100

#### 6.2 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Free, confidential support for employees and family members:
- Mental health counseling (up to 8 sessions per issue)
- Financial counseling
- Legal consultations
- Work-life resources (childcare, eldercare referrals)
- 24/7 crisis support line: 1-800-555-0199

#### 6.3 Fitness Benefits

- **Gym Membership Subsidy:** Up to $50/month reimbursement
- **On-site Fitness Center:** Free access at headquarters
- **Virtual Fitness Classes:** Free subscription to Contoso Fit app

### 7. ADDITIONAL BENEFITS

#### 7.1 Tuition Assistance

- **Annual Maximum:** $5,250 (tax-free under IRS limits)
- **Eligibility:** After 6 months of employment
- **Requirements:** Grade of B or better in job-related courses

#### 7.2 Commuter Benefits

- Pre-tax transit/parking deductions up to IRS limits
- Electric vehicle charging stations at all major offices

#### 7.3 Employee Discounts

- Contoso products: 25% discount
- Partner company discounts (electronics, travel, entertainment)
- Access through Contoso Perks portal

#### 7.4 Paid Volunteer Time

- **8 hours** of paid time annually for volunteer activities
- Team volunteer events organized quarterly

---

### 8. ENROLLMENT AND CHANGES

#### 8.1 New Hire Enrollment

Complete enrollment within **30 days** of your start date through the HR Portal.

#### 8.2 Open Enrollment

Annual open enrollment occurs each **November 1-15** for the following plan year.

#### 8.3 Qualifying Life Events

You may change benefits within 30 days of:
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status
