### 1. PURPOSE

This policy establishes guidelines for all types of employee leave at Contoso Corporation. It ensures consistent application of leave benefits while supporting employee work-life balance and compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

---

### 2. SCOPE

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Contoso Corporation and its subsidiaries. Temporary and contract employees should refer to their employment agreements for applicable leave provisions.

---

### 3. TYPES OF LEAVE

#### 3.1 Annual Leave (Vacation)

| Years of Service | Annual Entitlement |
|------------------|-------------------|
| 0 - 2 years | 15 days (120 hours) |
| 3 - 5 years | 20 days (160 hours) |
| 6 - 10 years | 25 days (200 hours) |
| 10+ years | 30 days (240 hours) |

**Accrual:** Annual leave accrues on a monthly basis (1/12 of annual entitlement per month).

**Carryover:** Employees may carry over a maximum of 5 unused days to the following calendar year. Carried-over days must be used by March 31 of the new year.

**Request Process:**
1. Submit leave request through the HR Portal at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Obtain manager approval before confirming travel arrangements
3. Ensure adequate coverage during absence

#### 3.2 Sick Leave

All employees are entitled to **12 days (96 hours)** of paid sick leave per calendar year.

**Usage Guidelines:**
- Sick leave may be used for personal illness, injury, or medical appointments
- Employees must notify their manager as early as possible on the first day of absence
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding **3 consecutive days**
- Unused sick leave does not carry over and is not paid out upon termination

#### 3.3 Personal Leave

Employees are entitled to **3 days (24 hours)** of personal leave per calendar year.

**Eligible Uses:**
- Personal matters requiring attention during work hours
- Family emergencies
- Religious observances not covered by company holidays
- Other personal commitments at manager's discretion

#### 3.4 Bereavement Leave

| Relationship | Paid Leave Entitlement |
|--------------|----------------------|
| Spouse, Child, Parent | 5 days |
| Sibling, Grandparent, In-law | 3 days |
| Extended Family Member | 1 day |

Additional unpaid leave may be approved at management discretion.

#### 3.5 Parental Leave

**Primary Caregiver:** 16 weeks of paid leave at 100% salary  
**Secondary Caregiver:** 4 weeks of paid leave at 100% salary

Parental leave applies to birth, adoption, or foster placement. Leave must commence within 12 months of the qualifying event.

---

### 4. LEAVE REQUEST PROCEDURES

1. **Submit Request:** Log in to the HR Portal (hrportal.contoso.com) and navigate to "My Leave"
2. **Select Type:** Choose the appropriate leave category
3. **Enter Dates:** Specify start and end dates
4. **Add Notes:** Include relevant information for your manager
5. **Submit:** Submit request for manager approval
6. **Confirmation:** You will receive email confirmation once approved

---

### 5. UNPAID LEAVE

Unpaid leave may be granted for extended personal circumstances. Requests must be submitted in writing to HR with at least 30 days notice when possible. Approval is at management discretion and subject to business needs.

---

### 6. LEAVE DURING PROBATION

Employees in their probationary period (first 90 days) may use accrued leave with manager approval. Probationary employees are not eligible for unpaid leave except in exceptional circumstances.

---

### 7. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS

Contoso Corporation observes all federal public holidays. When a holiday falls on a weekend, the preceding Friday or following Monday will be observed. Refer to the annual Holiday Calendar (HR-POL-002) for specific dates.

---

### 8. POLICY COMPLIANCE

Failure to comply with this policy, including falsification of leave records, may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.
