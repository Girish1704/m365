### WELCOME TO CONTOSO!

Congratulations on joining the Contoso team! We are excited to have you as part of our organization. This guide will help you navigate your first days, weeks, and months at Contoso, ensuring you have everything you need to succeed.

This onboarding guide provides new employees with essential information about Contoso Corporation, our culture, policies, and the resources available to help you thrive in your new role.

|------|------------|

| Week 2 | Role-specific training, understand processes |

#### 4.2 30-Day Checklist

**Administrative:**
- [ ] Complete all required training in Learning Portal
- [ ] Enroll in benefits (within 30 days of start)
- [ ] Set up direct deposit

- [ ] Review and acknowledge company policies

**Role-Specific:**

- [ ] Complete role-specific training

- [ ] Attend team meetings and social events
- [ ] Join relevant employee resource groups (ERGs)
- [ ] Connect with colleagues on Teams/LinkedIn

### 5. YOUR FIRST 90 DAYS (PROBATIONARY PERIOD)

#### 5.1 Expectations

During your probationary period, you will:
- Learn your role and responsibilities
- Demonstrate alignment with Contoso values
- Build relationships with colleagues and stakeholders
- Begin contributing meaningfully to team goals

#### 5.2 Key Milestones

| Milestone | Timing | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 30-Day Check-in | Week 4 | Informal feedback conversation with manager |
| 60-Day Check-in | Week 8 | Progress review, address any concerns |
| 90-Day Review | Week 12 | Formal probation completion review |

#### 5.3 Probation Completion

At the end of 90 days, your manager will:
- Confirm successful completion of probation, OR
- Extend probation (if additional development needed), OR
- Determine the role is not a good fit

Successful completion unlocks:
- Full leave accrual
- Eligibility for transfers and promotions
- Long-term disability insurance
