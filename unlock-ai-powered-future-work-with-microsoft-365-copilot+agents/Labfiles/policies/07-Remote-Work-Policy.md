# CONTOSO CORPORATION

## REMOTE WORK AND FLEXIBLE WORKING POLICY

**Document Number:** HR-POL-007  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Reviewed:** December 1, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department  
**Approved By:** Sarah Mitchell, Chief Human Resources Officer

---

### 1. PURPOSE

This policy establishes guidelines for remote work and flexible working arrangements at Contoso Corporation. We recognize that flexibility in when and where work is performed can enhance productivity, work-life balance, and employee satisfaction while maintaining business effectiveness.

---

### 2. SCOPE

This policy applies to all regular employees of Contoso Corporation. Eligibility for specific flexible arrangements depends on role requirements, performance, and business needs.

---

### 3. WORK ARRANGEMENT OPTIONS

#### 3.1 Work Models

Contoso offers three primary work models:

| Model | Description | Office Requirement |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| **On-Site** | Work primarily from Contoso office | 5 days per week |
| **Hybrid** | Combination of office and remote work | 2-3 days per week |
| **Remote** | Work primarily from home/alternative location | As needed (typically monthly) |

#### 3.2 Role Classification

Each role is classified based on business requirements:

| Classification | Eligible Models | Examples |
|----------------|-----------------|----------|
| **On-Site Required** | On-Site only | Lab technicians, facilities, reception |
| **Hybrid Eligible** | On-Site or Hybrid | Most corporate roles |
| **Remote Eligible** | Any model | Customer support, some engineering roles |

Check with your manager or HR for your role's classification.

---

### 4. ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

To be eligible for remote or hybrid work:

- [ ] Completed probationary period (90 days)
- [ ] Performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or above
- [ ] Role is classified as hybrid or remote eligible
- [ ] Manager approval obtained
- [ ] Suitable home workspace established
- [ ] Reliable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps download)

---

### 5. REQUESTING FLEXIBLE ARRANGEMENTS

#### 5.1 Request Process

1. **Discuss** with your manager informally
2. **Submit** formal request through HR Portal
3. **Review** by manager within 10 business days
4. **Decision** communicated in writing
5. **Agreement** signed if approved

#### 5.2 Trial Period

New flexible arrangements include a **90-day trial period** to assess effectiveness. The arrangement may be modified or discontinued if:
- Performance declines
- Business needs change
- Communication or collaboration issues arise
- Employee requests return to previous arrangement

---

### 6. REMOTE WORK REQUIREMENTS

#### 6.1 Workspace Requirements

Remote employees must maintain a workspace that is:
- Safe and ergonomic
- Free from distractions during work hours
- Secure for confidential work
- Equipped with reliable internet

#### 6.2 Equipment and Technology

| Item | Provided By | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Laptop/Computer | Contoso | Company-issued devices required |
| Monitor | Employee (or Contoso for full-time remote) | Reimbursement available |
| Keyboard/Mouse | Employee | Reimbursement available |
| Internet | Employee | Must meet minimum requirements |
| Office Chair | Employee | Ergonomic subsidy available |
| Phone | Contoso (if required) | Teams/Softphone preferred |

#### 6.3 Home Office Stipend

Remote and hybrid employees receive:
- **Initial Setup:** $500 one-time (full remote) / $250 (hybrid)
- **Monthly Stipend:** $75 (full remote) / $50 (hybrid)
- Covers internet, utilities, office supplies

---

### 7. WORKING HOURS AND AVAILABILITY

#### 7.1 Core Hours

All employees, regardless of location, must be available during:
- **Core Hours:** 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (local time)

Outside core hours, flexible scheduling is permitted with manager agreement.

#### 7.2 Expectations

Remote employees are expected to:
- Maintain regular communication with team
- Respond to messages within reasonable time (typically 2 hours during work hours)
- Attend all required meetings (video on when practical)
- Update calendar with availability and work location
- Be reachable via Teams, email, or phone during work hours

---

### 8. COMMUNICATION AND COLLABORATION

#### 8.1 Required Practices

| Practice | Expectation |
|----------|-------------|
| **Daily Stand-ups** | Participate via video (if applicable to team) |
| **Calendar** | Keep updated with work location, meetings, focus time |
| **Status** | Use Teams status accurately (Available, Busy, DND) |
| **Video Meetings** | Camera on for team meetings when practical |
| **Documentation** | Document decisions and share via accessible channels |

#### 8.2 In-Office Days (Hybrid)

Hybrid employees should coordinate office days with their team to maximize collaboration. Common practices:
- Team meetings scheduled on common in-office days
- Minimum 2 days in office per week
- Specific days may be required by manager

---

### 9. SECURITY AND CONFIDENTIALITY

#### 9.1 Security Requirements

Remote employees must:
- Use only Contoso-issued devices for work
- Connect via VPN when accessing company systems
- Lock devices when not in use
- Keep screens shielded from others when working with confidential information
- Not use public Wi-Fi without VPN

#### 9.2 Confidential Information

- Do not discuss sensitive matters where others can overhear
- Use headphones for confidential calls
- Secure physical documents and dispose of properly
- Report any security incidents immediately to IT Security

---

### 10. HEALTH AND SAFETY

#### 10.1 Ergonomics

Contoso provides:
- Ergonomic assessment (virtual) upon request
- Guidance on proper workstation setup
- Subsidies for ergonomic equipment

#### 10.2 Work-Related Injuries

Injuries occurring during work hours at your home office may be covered by workers' compensation. Report any incidents immediately to your manager and HR.

#### 10.3 Mental Health

Working remotely can present unique challenges. Contoso supports your well-being through:
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Virtual wellness programs
- Encouraged breaks and boundaries
- Manager training on supporting remote teams

---

### 11. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

Remote and hybrid employees are held to the same performance standards as on-site employees.

| Aspect | Approach |
|--------|----------|
| **Goals** | Clear, measurable objectives |
| **Measurement** | Based on outcomes, not hours |
| **Feedback** | Regular 1:1s (minimum bi-weekly) |
| **Reviews** | Standard performance review process |

---

### 12. EXPENSES AND REIMBURSEMENT

#### 12.1 Reimbursable Expenses

| Expense | Eligibility | Limit |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Internet service | Hybrid/Remote | $50/month |
| Ergonomic equipment | All remote eligible | $300 one-time |
| Office supplies | All | Reasonable expenses |
| Travel to office | Remote employees (if required) | Company policy |

#### 12.2 Non-Reimbursable

- Home office furniture (beyond ergonomic subsidy)
- Home utilities (covered by monthly stipend)
- Personal devices or software
- Coworking space (unless pre-approved)

---

### 13. INTERNATIONAL REMOTE WORK

Working from outside your assigned country requires:
- **Prior approval** from HR and Legal
- **Maximum duration:** 4 weeks per calendar year
- **Tax and compliance** review
- **Security clearance** for certain roles

Extended international work may affect tax status and benefits. Consult HR before making arrangements.

---

### 14. CHANGES AND TERMINATION

#### 14.1 Modifying Arrangements

Flexible work arrangements may be modified due to:
- Changes in role or responsibilities
- Business needs or restructuring
- Performance concerns
- Employee request

30 days notice will be provided when possible.

#### 14.2 Returning Equipment

Upon termination or change to on-site role, remote equipment must be returned within 5 business days. Contoso will provide shipping labels and instructions.
