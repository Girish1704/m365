## CASE SUMMARY

Michael Johnson, a 15-year employee of Apex Manufacturing, alleges wrongful termination based on age discrimination. Mr. Johnson (age 58) claims he was terminated as part of a "restructuring" while younger, less experienced employees were retained.

### Key Allegations:
1. Violation of Age Discrimination in Employment Act (ADEA)
2. Pattern of age-based comments by supervisors
3. Failure to follow company termination procedures
4. Disparate treatment compared to younger employees
