# Mission to Transform Contoso Corp with Microsoft 365 Copilot and Agents 

#### Estimated Duration: 180 Minutes

## Overview

This Immersive experience places participants in the role of a newly hired Transformation Lead at Contoso Corp, a fast-growing services company in operational chaos. Participants will use Microsoft 365 Copilot, free web-grounded Copilot Chat, and Copilot Studio Agents to solve real-world business challenges across departments like Support, HR, Leadership, Sales, and Procurement. 

With a workflow-driven narrative and role-based tasks, this lab offers a highly interactive experience that showcases the value of AI-powered assistance in streamlining work, accelerating decision-making, and empowering teams.

## **Objective**

The objective of the lab is to enable participants to utilize Microsoft 365 Copilot and Copilot Studio to enhance the processes and workflows. The lab aims to demonstrate how AI can streamline various tasks, improve data analysis, and support decision-making, ultimately leading to more efficient and effective Leadership, HR, Sales, Procure management. By the end of this course, participants will be able to:

1. **The Spark – Identifying Chaos**
2. **Leadership Misalignment**
3. **Support Chaos – AI-Powered Customer Issue Resolution**
4. **HR Onboarding Crisis**
5. **Scaling with Procurement Agent – AI-Powered Procurement(Optional)**
6. **Sales & Marketing Fire Drill(Optional)**

## Prerequisites

Participants should have basic knowledge and understanding of the following:

- Basic understanding of Microsoft 365, Microsoft 365 Copilot, Prompts and Microsoft Copilot Studio.
